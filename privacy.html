Privacy Statement

This privacy statement discloses the privacy practices for our petition website.

Information Collection and Use

We collect information from you when you sign up to create or sign a petition on our website. We may collect your name, email address, and other information related to your petition or signature. We use this information to communicate with you about your petition and to send you updates about our website.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with any third-party companies or organizations. We may, however, share your petition and signature information publicly on our website.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your user experience. These cookies do not contain any personal information about you.

Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and disclosure.

Changes to this Privacy Statement

We may update this privacy statement from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy statement on our website.
