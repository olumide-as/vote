Frequently Asked Questions

The purpose of this petition is to call for an end to unfair employment practices that discriminate against employees based on their gender, race, religion, age, disability, or other protected characteristics. We want to raise awareness about these issues and demand that employers take steps to create a safe and fair workplace for all employees.

By signing this petition, you are adding your voice to a growing movement that is calling for an end to unfair employment practices. The more signatures we gather, the more impact we can have in raising awareness about these issues and putting pressure on employers to take action.

Anyone who supports the cause and wants to see an end to unfair employment practices can sign this petition. Whether you are an employee, employer, or concerned citizen, your voice is important in this movement.

Your personal information, such as your name and email address, will be kept private and will not be shared with any third parties. However, your name may be displayed on the petition website along with other supporters of the cause.
